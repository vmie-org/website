### Tech stack
At <a href="https://vmie.org">vmie.org</a> we embrace a KISS approach on technology.

Backend: **.Net8**
Frontend: **Vue 2** without a build step.

Styling: Keep it minimal. Reuse when possible. Avoid inline styleing.
Markdown: The main content of all pages if markdown. Easy to edit.

All public projects are MIT license on <a href="https://github.com/vmie-org/website">GitHub</a>
